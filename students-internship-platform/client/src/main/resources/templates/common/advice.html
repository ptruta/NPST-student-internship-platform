Our Advices

Hello! Here you can receive an advice of how to create your RESUME in order to apply to the internships or announcements of jobs available.

If you will listen to our advice, your RESUME will be accepted easily by any company.

Here are 4 steps which will help you create a great RESUME:

1. First, you can have a specific template of RESUME in which you can construct your resume. Until we will have that feature in our application for giving specific templates, you can use a few of them which are already available online like: resume.io, canvas.com

2. Second, you need to think about what makes you out of common.

3. After making an honest opinion about you, you will need to write down a list containing the following step.

4. The content of the list should be displayed on this order: Education, Work Experience, Skills, Personal Projects, Spare Time Activity, Volunteering Work
