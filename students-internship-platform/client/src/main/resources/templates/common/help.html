The purpose of this specification is to provide a set of documentation that describes the features and behavior of a software application for organising, searching and finding internships.

The readership of the current document may consist of system end-users, client engineers, system architects or software developers. All the information will be shared in such a manner that any person that is targeted to have access to this paper might be able to comprehend without issues.

The application has the goal of easily providing stakeholders with all the information and functionalities they demand when looking for an internship opportunity. The requirements that will furthermore be presented in this document help the readers understand what are the needs that this software application will supply.

Throughout the information that is presented here it can be found a detailed description of what functionalities should be implemented.

The software system to be produced is a Students Internship Platform, which will be referred to as “HollyStart” throughout this document.

The platform will offer functionalities for facilitating the communication between the employer and the employee, particularly the students audience.

The objective of the HollyStart is to record the internship announcements posted by companies from which a student may choose to apply to. The goals are multiple depending on the users of the HollyStart.

The platform is designed to be used by two types of users: the applicant and the company representative(recruiter). The recruiter can post internship announcements, can edit his account, can delete internship announcements and edit internship announcements, while the applicant has the possibility to surf the announcements when interested, apply for an internship, edit his/her account. Both types of users will have to make an account(to register) in order to use the platform.

The profile of each user will contain relevant information which will interest the other party and also make the entire recruitment process easier.
